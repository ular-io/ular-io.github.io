Common Questions

Where is my data stored? Your data is stored exclusively on your local device in a secure SQLite database. We never upload your files or metadata to any external servers. Does UlarBox support cloud sync? No. UlarBox is built on a "local-first" principle to ensure maximum privacy, speed, and offline availability. You have full control over your own data. What file types can I manage? UlarBox supports a wide range of formats including Images, Videos, Audio, PDF, Markdown, and plain text. If it's a file on your disk, UlarBox can help you organize it. How do I backup my database? You can easily export your entire box configuration and metadata using the built-in Export feature for safe backup and manual migration.
